Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 27, 2026

Protecting your privacy is important to us. We hope the following statement will help you understand how ‘App Tapp Studios’ deals with the personal identifiable information ('PII') you may occasionally provide to us via the Internet.

Generally, we do not collect any PII from you when you download our iOS applications. To be specific, we do not require the consumers to get registered before downloading the application. We do keep track of how many times our application is visited in general, but this information is not linked to you.

App Tapp Studios uses AppLovin as an ads provider. AppLovin wants to show ads that are relevant to you. For more information, please check: https://www.applovin.com/privacy/

If you have any concern about your PII being misused, or if you want further information about our privacy policy and what it means, please feel free to email us at support@apptappstudios.com, we will endeavor to provide clear answers to your questions in a timely manner.
